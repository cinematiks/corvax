Axcess Capital Advisors Inc. is the registered exempt market dealer through which exempt market securities transactions are facilitated. John P. Sao Miguel is a dealing representative of Axcess Capital Advisors Inc., operating under the trade name Corvax Financial.

2. Products and Services We Offer

Corvax Financial / John P. Sao Miguel, through Axcess Capital Advisors Inc., offers the following services:

  • Facilitating the purchase of exempt market securities, including private placements, limited partnership interests, mortgage investment corporations, real estate investment trusts, and other securities sold pursuant to prospectus exemptions
  • Conducting know-your-client (KYC) assessments and suitability reviews
  • Providing information and analysis on specific exempt market offerings

We do not provide portfolio management, investment advice regarding publicly traded securities, insurance products, tax advice, or legal advice. For these services, you should consult qualified professionals.

3. Know Your Client and Suitability

Before recommending any exempt market investment, we are required under Alberta securities legislation to collect sufficient information about you to assess whether the investment is suitable for your circumstances. This includes:

  • Your financial situation, including income, assets, liabilities, and liquidity needs
  • Your investment objectives (income, capital preservation, growth, or a combination)
  • Your risk tolerance
  • Your investment time horizon
  • Your investment knowledge and experience

You are responsible for providing accurate, complete, and current information. If your circumstances change materially, please notify us promptly so that we can update your profile and reassess suitability.

We will not recommend an investment if, based on the information available to us, we believe it is unsuitable for you.

4. How We Are Compensated

Dealing commissions

We receive dealing commissions paid by issuers of exempt market securities. These commissions are calculated as a percentage of the amount you invest and are disclosed in the offering memorandum for each investment. You do not pay us directly — the commission is paid by the issuer from the proceeds of the offering.

Trailer fees

In some cases, we may receive ongoing trailer fees or service commissions paid by the issuer for as long as you hold the investment. Where applicable, these will be disclosed to you prior to investment.

Co-investment

John P. Sao Miguel may invest personal capital in the same offerings recommended to clients. Where this occurs, the terms of his investment are identical to yours. This co-investment is disclosed to you as part of the investment recommendation process.

No additional fees to you

We do not charge advisory fees, retainer fees, or account management fees to clients. Our compensation comes entirely from issuers as described above, and all compensation is disclosed in the relevant offering memorandum.

5. Conflicts of Interest

We are committed to identifying, disclosing, and managing conflicts of interest in a manner that prioritises your interests. The following conflicts exist or may arise:

  • Issuer-paid compensation: Because dealing commissions are paid by issuers, we have a financial incentive to recommend investments. We manage this by conducting independent due diligence, declining offerings that do not meet our standards, and disclosing all compensation to you before you invest.
  • Co-investment: Where we invest personal capital alongside clients, our interests are aligned. However, the dollar amount of our investment may differ from yours, which could affect incentives in certain scenarios involving early redemption or exit decisions.
  • Multiple clients in the same offering: We may recommend the same offering to multiple clients. This does not create a material conflict but is disclosed for transparency.
  • Relationship with Axcess Capital Advisors Inc.: As a dealing representative of Axcess Capital Advisors Inc., we operate within their compliance framework and may only deal in securities approved by Axcess Capital Advisors Inc.

Where a material conflict of interest arises that is not described above, we will disclose it to you promptly and explain how we intend to manage it.

6. Risks of Exempt Market Investing

Exempt market investments carry significant risks that differ from publicly traded securities. Key risks include:

  • Illiquidity: There is generally no secondary market for exempt market securities. You should be prepared to hold your investment for the full term, which may be several years.
  • Loss of capital: You may lose some or all of your invested capital. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
  • No prospectus: Exempt market securities are not subject to the same disclosure requirements as prospectus-qualified securities.
  • Reliance on management: The performance of many exempt market investments depends heavily on the skill and integrity of the issuer's management team.
  • Concentration risk: Investing in a small number of exempt market offerings may result in a concentrated portfolio.

For a full discussion of risks, please review our Risk Disclosure page and the risk factors section of the applicable offering memorandum.

7. Your Rights as an Investor

Under Alberta securities legislation, investors who purchase securities under the offering memorandum exemption have statutory rights of rescission and damages in certain circumstances where the offering memorandum contains a misrepresentation. The specific terms of these rights are described in the offering memorandum for each investment.
